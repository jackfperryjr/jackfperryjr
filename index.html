About Me
Jack F. Perry, Jr.

I'm a digital artist and photography is a hobby of mine. I'm also a developer and love creating beautiful images and organizing and manipulating them on the web!

Born in Northeast Ohio and currently residing in Louisville, Kentucky. I started drawing since before I can remember. During my teen years (1995-ish!) I took to the web and started building web sites, on my own, using Yahoo! Geocities; do you remember that!?

Then I got into gaming and got lazy. But, now I've completed an Associate's in Applied Science in Computer Programming. I'm also a student with Code Louisville and I continue to teach myself new programming languages, technologies, and best practices on a daily basis. The learning never stops!
